# Spec: Refactor

## Problem Statement
After the midterm review, feedback from the professor and curator evaluation will identify specific improvements. This workstream captures those improvements for implementation before the final review.

## Acceptance Criteria
To be filled in after midterm review feedback is received.

## Scope
To be defined after midterm review.

## Dependencies
- Midterm review must be complete
- All other workstreams must be complete

## Notes
This workstream is intentionally empty until feedback is received. Its presence signals that the process anticipates iteration, not just initial delivery.
